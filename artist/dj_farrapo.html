Italian dj, musician and producer. His solid reputation as a dj has seen him perform in clubs from Italy, Austria, Germany, England, Portugal, Spain, Bulgaria, Polonia, Hungaria, Slovakia, Czech Republic, Greece, Belgium, France, Switzerland, Denmark, Brasil.

His research and production style is influenced by his love of traditional music from all over the world and he has perfected a unique blend of Balkan, Brazilian and Afro sounds mixed up with contemporary dancefloor rhythms such as drum’n’bass, broken beat & break beat.

He remixed well known bands and artists like The New Mastersound, Ojos De Brujos, Solo Moderna, Roy Paci, Janko Nilovic, Makala, Municiplae Balcanica…

Press
